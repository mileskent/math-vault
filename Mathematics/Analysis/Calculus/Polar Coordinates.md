# Conversion
Polar to Cartesian
$$
x = r\cos(\theta)
\quad\quad
y = r\sin(\theta)
$$
Cartesian to Polar
$$
r^2 = x^2 + y^2
\quad\quad
$$

# Double Integral
$$
\iint f(x,y)\ dA = \int^{\theta=\beta}_{\theta=\alpha} \int^{r=h_{2}(\theta)}_{r=h_{1}(\theta)} f(r\cos(\theta), r \sin(\theta))\ r \ dr d\theta
$$
