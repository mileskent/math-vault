If $\vec{T}(u, v)$ is a [[Linear Transformation#1-1]] [[Differentiable]] [[Transformation]] that maps a region $G$ in the uv-plane to a region $R$ in the xy-plane and $\vec{T}(u_{0}, v_{0}) = (x_{0},y_{0})$, then we have
$$
\left|\det(D \vec{T}(u_{0}, v_{0}))\right|
=
\frac{1}{\left|\det(D \vec{T}^{-1}(x_{0}, y_{0}))\right|}
$$
