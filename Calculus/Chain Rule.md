# Total Derivative
See [[Total Derivative]]
$f : \mathbb{R}^p \to \mathbb{R}^m$ and $g : \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}^p$
$$
D(f(g(x))) = Df(g(x)) \, Dg(x)
$$


# 1 dimensional
$$
(f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)
$$
# Flat Multidimensional
$$
(f(x(a,b,c), y(a,b,c), z(a,b,c)))_c = f_{x}x_{c} + f_{y}y_{c}+f_{z}z_{c}
$$
