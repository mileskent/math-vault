$$
W_{k}(ze^z) = z,\quad z \in S_{k} \subset \mathbb{C}
$$
where $S_{k}$ is the range of the $k^\text{th}$ branch of $W$, i.e. the branch of $W_{k}$
# Example
$$
\begin{gathered}
A^x + x = B,\quad \\
A^x = B - x\\
1 = (B-x)A^{-x}\\
A^B = (B-x)A^{B-x}\\
A^B \cdot \ln A = \ln A (B-x)e^{\ln A (B-x)}\\
W(A^B \cdot \ln A) = W\left(\ln A (B-x)e^{\ln A (B-x)}\right)\\
W(A^B \cdot \ln A) = \ln A (B-x)\\
x = B - \frac{W(A^B \cdot \ln A)}{\ln A}
\end{gathered}
$$
