A group $G$ is a [[Set]] that satisfies the axioms of a group.
# Axioms
1. [[Closure]]: $A, B \in G \implies AB \in G$
2. [[Associativity]]: $A, B, C \in G \implies (AB)C = A(BC)$
3. [[Identity]]: $\exists\ I \text{ s.t. } \forall A \in G,\ IA = AI = A$
4. [[Inverse]]: $\forall\ A \in S,\ A^{-1} \in S \text{ s.t. } AA^{-1} = A^{-1}A = I$
