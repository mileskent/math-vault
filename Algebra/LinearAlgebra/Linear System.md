A system of [[Linear Equation]]s.

# Representation
There are several equivalent ways to represent a linear system.

A system of [[Equation|Equations]]
$$
\begin{align}
2x+3y &= 7\\
x-y &= 5
\end{align}\\
$$
An [[Augmented Matrix]]
$$
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
2 & 3 & 7 \\
1 & -1 & 5
\end{array}\right]
$$
A [[Vector Equation]]
$$
x 
\begin{bmatrix}
2 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
+
y
\begin{bmatrix}
3 \\
-1
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
7 \\
5
\end{bmatrix}
$$
A [[Matrix Equation]]
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
2 & 3 \\
1 & -1
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
7 \\
5
\end{bmatrix}
$$
