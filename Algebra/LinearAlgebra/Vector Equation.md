An [[Equation]] comprised of [[Vector]]s, rather than [[Scalar]]s.

For example, the vector equation of a [[Space Curve]] through $\mathbb{R}^3$
$$
\begin{gathered}
\vec{r}(t) = \vec{r}_{0} + t\vec{\mathbf{v}} \\
\vec{r}_{0},\ \vec{\mathbf{v}} \in \mathbb{R}^3
\quad t \in \mathbb{R}
\end{gathered}
$$
