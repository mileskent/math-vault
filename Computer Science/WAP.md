A [[Open Systems Interconnection Model#Physical Layer]] [[Networking Device]] serving as a central [[Network Node|Node]] for at least two other wireless nodes. A WAP can operate as a standalone device or act as a [[Bridge]] between a [[Local Area Network|LAN]] and a [[Wireless Local Area Network|WLAN]]. A WAP provides [[Switch]]-like capabilities to wireless networked devices (layer 2)
