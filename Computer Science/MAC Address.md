a unique 48-bit identifier burned into a [[Network Interface Card|Network Interface Controller]]
First 3 bytes are the Organizationally Unique Identifier
* Who manufactured it? 
* e.g. Apple -> 00:25:00, Oracle -> 00:03:BA
* OUI lookup [here](https://www.wireshark.org/tools/oui-lookup.html)
The remaining are unique to the [[Network Interface Card|NIC]]
