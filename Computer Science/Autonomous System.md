A collection systems ([[Host]]s) under [[IP]] routing [[Prefix]]es, under one administrative domain that presents a single, consistent [[Routing Protocol]] to the rest of the [[Internet]] using [[Border Gateway Protocol|BGP]].
* AS's are connected together by AS [[Gateway]]s.
* [[Border Gateway Protocol|BGP]] connects AS's
* [[Distance-Vector Algorithm]] or [[Link-State Algorithm]] connects within an AS.
