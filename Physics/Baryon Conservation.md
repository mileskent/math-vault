A conservation law of [[Quantum Mechanics]] that states:
	The number of [[Baryon]]s is always conserved
Used for particle interaction
# Baryon Number
$$
B = [-1, 0, 1]
$$
* $B = 1$ is for baryons
* $B = -1$ is for antibaryons
* $B = 0$ for all other particles, e.g. [[Meson]]s, [[Lepton]]s
