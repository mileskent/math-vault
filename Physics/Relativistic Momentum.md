$$
\vec{p} 
= \gamma_{\text{total}} \ m \vec{v}
$$
$$
\gamma_{\text{total}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1 - \frac{\text{v}_{\text{total}}^2}{c^2} }}
$$

For massless particles
$$p = \frac{E}{c} = \frac{h\nu}{c} = \frac{h}{\lambda} = \hbar k$$
