$$
E_{\text{total}} = K + E_{0} = \gamma mc^2
$$
where $E_{0} = mc^2$ is the "rest energy"; literally the energy when the object is at rest
$$
K= (\gamma - 1)mc^2 = \gamma mc^2 - mc^2 = \gamma mc^2 - E_{0} 
$$

$$
p^2c^2 = E_{\text{total}}^2-E_{0}^2
$$
