A part of a [[Set]]; Set A is a subset of B.

# Definition
A set $A$ is a subset of $B$ if all elements of A are also elements of B.
$$
A \subseteq B \iff \forall x\ (x \in A \implies x \in B)
$$

# Proper Subset
$$
A \subset B \equiv A \subseteq B \land A \neq B
$$
