To find a center of mass $(\bar{x}, \bar{y}, \bar{z})$
$$
(\bar{x}, \bar{y}, \bar{z}) = \left( \frac{M_{yz}}{M}, \frac{M_{xz}}{M}, \frac{M_{xy}}{M} \right)
$$
where
$$
M = \iiint_{E} \delta(x,y,z)\ dV
$$
$$
M_{yz} = \iiint_{E} x \cdot \delta(x,y,z)\ dV
$$
$$
M_{xz} = \iiint_{E} y \cdot \delta(x,y,z)\ dV
$$
$$
M_{xy} = \iiint_{E} z \cdot \delta(x,y,z)\ dV
$$
and $\delta$ is a density function
